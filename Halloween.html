Halloween history, legends, and spooky stories

Halloween in 2025 falls on Friday, October 31. People decorate with spooky themes and dress as ghosts or monsters. Carved pumpkin lanterns appear everywhere.

It has ancient origins but is now celebrated widely, including in Thailand as highlighted by Thai Rath Online.

The festival likely began with the Celts in Ireland. Their new year began on November 1, celebrating Samhain, the god of death. On October 31, they believed spirits returned to the living world.

To stay safe, people hid in the dark and dressed like spirits so ghosts would ignore them. These customs evolved into todayâ€™s Halloween.

The carved pumpkin tradition comes from the story of Jack O'Lantern, a trickster who trapped a demon. Rejected from heaven and hell, he wandered with burning coal inside a hollow turnip. This became the glowing pumpkin lantern we use today.

Modern Halloween is filled with decorations, costumes, pumpkin carving, parties, and trick-or-treating.
